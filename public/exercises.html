When someone wants to start exercising, the big question is "What exercises should I do". It's hard to give a perfect answer as the needs of everyone is different. However, here you can find some ideas to start your program or expand it

Maybe you don't have tools to train. It's alright, there is plenty of exercises you can do using your bodyweight only. Everybody knows push-ups or squats but there is other overlooked exercises like lunges or planks. They are simple to execute and activate many muscles at the same time, making them useful for daily fitness or warm-ups. Additionally, you can get your hands on dumbbells or a gym ball. They don't take much room and can open a wide range of exercises.

Training outside

While you're doing your exercises, you may want to go outside. If so, take the opportunity to go for a short run: it's always a good way to train your cardio. If it's your first time you may settle for a jogging but the most dedicated of you can try interval running to improve your performances.

Another good outdoor exercise is swimming. Altough it may not always be intense, swimming activates all muscles of the body. It's also less stressful for the body, which is helpful for people with an injury. Be careful however to always swim in a supervised environnement.

Those exercises are only a suggestion. Feel free to add your own exercises which may suit your project.
